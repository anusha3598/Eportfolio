Image Editing

Book Cover

For this project, I designed a cover for The Devil Wears Prada. The minimalist black background with a bold red title reflects the story's intense and stylish themes.To emphasize the novel's widespread acclaim, I added a "#1 New York Times Bestseller" badge. Tools like Canva allowed me to experiment with layout and design principles, creating a clean and balanced composition.Through this project, I gained valuable insights into the principles of design, such as the effective use of typography, imagery, and layout to convey a story.

Logo creation

Inspired by my love for coffee and nature, this logo blends the elements of a steaming coffee cup and blooming flowers. Earthy brown tones evoke coffee’s richness, while blue accents add freshness, symbolizing a nurturing and inviting café environment.This project taught me how to align design elements with brand identity, from selecting the right color scheme to balancing typography and imagery, creating a logo that reflects a brand's values and mission.
