Image Editing

Book Cover

PART 1

I designed a book cover for The Devil Wears Prada, a novel that explores ambition, relationships, and personal growth through the journey of Andrea Sachs, an assistant to the powerful Miranda Priestly at Runway magazine.

For the design, I chose a minimalist black background with a subtle gradient, reflecting the fashion industry’s elegance. The title, The Devil Wears Prada, is styled in bold red for power, with Lauren Weisberger’s name in a smaller, elegant font.

I added a "#1 New York Times Bestseller" badge to highlight its success. The main image features a stylish woman symbolizing Miranda Priestly, with devil horns and a tail to hint at her complex nature.

The layout is clean and balanced, ensuring harmony between the title, imagery, and author’s name, with enough white space to keep the design sophisticated and minimalist.

Logo

PART 2

I designed a logo for a café I hope to open, combining my love for coffee and nature. The logo features a coffee cup with a blooming flower emerging from it, symbolizing growth and freshness.

The color scheme includes earthy brown tones for coffee and soft blue for freshness. The name "Bloom & Brew" is in bold, modern typography, with "Coffee Shop" beneath in a smaller font. Basic vector shapes like circles and lines were used to keep the design simple and clean.
