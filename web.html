My Web Design Projects

Book-to-Genre Quiz

The Literary Matchmaking Drag-and-Drop Quiz invites users to match popular books like Gone Girl and Snow Crash with their genres. Featuring a sleek design inspired by bookshelves, the quiz uses draggable items and grid-arranged genre boxes with subtle shadows for a library-like feel. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it offers smooth functionality and an interactive experience for book lovers.

Coffee Quiz

Freshly Brewed Quiz is a playful and dynamic interactive quiz crafted for coffee enthusiasts. It features 10 thoughtfully curated questions blending humorous, witty prompts with fascinating coffee facts. Designed with a coffee-centric aesthetic, it creates a visually immersive experience for users.
