My Web Design Projects

In this project, I designed two interactive quizzes: Literary Matchmaking Drag-and-Drop Quiz and a Freshly Brewed Quiz, each offering a unique and engaging experience for users. Both projects allowed me to enhance my skills in creative design and interactive web development, combining storytelling with technical expertise to deliver engaging user experiences. Whether testing coffee knowledge or diving into the literary world, these quizzes highlight my ability to merge functionality with aesthetics for diverse audiences.

The Literary Matchmaking Drag-and-Drop Quiz invites users to match popular books like Gone Girl and Snow Crash with their genres. Featuring a sleek design inspired by bookshelves, the quiz uses draggable items and grid-arranged genre boxes with subtle shadows for a library-like feel. Dynamic borders provide immediate feedback on correct or incorrect matches, ensuring an interactive experience. Built with JavaScript and CSS, the quiz offers smooth functionality and a user-friendly interface, combining creativity and technical skill in a fun activity for book lovers.
