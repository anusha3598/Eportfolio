Friction Animation Project

  • By designing an animation, I bridged the gap between abstract ideas and tangible understanding, showcasing how friction impacts motion through clear and dynamic visuals.
  • The process aligns with modern educational practices, emphasizing interactive and multimedia tools to enhance student engagement and comprehension.
  • I drafted a concise script, ensuring the content was focused and suitable for a short video.
  • Using Adobe Animate was both exciting and challenging:
    • Designing elements like the rolling ball and simulating friction effects required attention to detail and precision.
    • The software offered powerful tools for customization but had a steep learning curve, especially when managing multiple layers and animations simultaneously.
  • I supplemented my work with Canva for additional design elements and transitions:
    • Canva's user-friendly interface helped me create polished slides and integrate visuals seamlessly.
    • However, ensuring consistency in style and timing between Canva and Adobe Animate was a challenge.
  • Recording the voiceover and syncing it with animations required multiple adjustments to achieve smooth narration.
