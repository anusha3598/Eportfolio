Hi there! I’m Anusha, a Master’s student in Computer Science at the University of Florida, passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world challenges. My journey began in India, where I earned my undergraduate degree in computer science and gained hands-on experience as a software developer. This experience sharpened my problem-solving abilities and fueled my confidence in tackling complex tech challenges. My technical expertise includes Python, Java, SQL, and web development. Outside the world of tech, i love dogs, a good cup of coffee, traveling to new places, painting and experiencing diverse cultures. As part of my Master’s coursework, I’m enrolled in EME 6208: Emerging Learning Technology, where I’m advancing my skills in image editing, web design, video production, screencasting, and digital animation. This course is helping me craft a personal e-portfolio that beautifully reflects the synergy between my technical skills and creative passion. I’m excited to connect, share my journey, and showcase the work that inspires my love for technology and creativity!
